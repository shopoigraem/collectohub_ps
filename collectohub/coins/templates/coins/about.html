About CollectoHub

Welcome to CollectoHub!

CollectoHub is a unique platform designed for coin enthusiasts and collectors to exchange collectible coins. We offer a safe and convenient way to trade coins, allowing you to enrich your collection with new and rare pieces.

How Does It Work?

  • Registration:

    Start by registering on our platform. This will give you access to your personal cabinet, where you can manage your coins, track exchanges, and interact with other members.

  • Sending Your Coins:

    After registering, you can send the coins you wish to trade to our mailing address. We take great care of your collection, ensuring each coin is photographed and described in detail.

  • Photography and Description:

    Once we receive your coins, we professionally photograph them and provide detailed descriptions. These images and descriptions are then uploaded to your personal cabinet, so you can easily manage your offers and view available trading options.

  • Trading Process:

    You can trade coins either with me or with other members of the platform. We offer a wide variety of coins to meet the interests of every collector.

  • Receiving Your Traded Coins:

    When you're ready to receive your traded coins, you’ll need to pay for the photography and exchange services. You can also opt to pay a single combined fee for both services. Once payment is received, we will promptly send your updated collection back to you.

Service Fees

We understand that every collection is unique, and we strive to offer our services at a reasonable price. Currently, our service fees are as follows:

  • Coin Photography: £0.15 per coin
  • Exchange Service: £0.35 per successful trade

About the Creator

My name is Danyil, and I am a passionate coin collector with years of experience. I created CollectoHub to bring together those who share my love for coins, and to provide a space where everyone can find something unique for their collection. My goal was to make the trading process safe, transparent, and convenient.

If you have any questions or would like to learn more, please don’t hesitate to reach out via email or through the contact form on the site. I’m always happy to help!
